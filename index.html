artist statement

The tools and systems that we use influence how we work individually and collectively, slowing us down to think more critically or hastening us towards fluid expression. I’m drawn to the processes that occur between a thought and an action. My work is interested in building systems that I can use to explore my relationship with myself and others, investigating themes of tedium, fixation, OCD, and connection using audio, graphics, and improvisation.
