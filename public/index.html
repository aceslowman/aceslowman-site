artist statement

I’m drawn towards tools and interfaces and how they impact outcomes. The tools and systems that we use decide how we move towards our goals, they can impede us to think more critically or hasten us towards fluid expression. My work is interested in the space between an idea’s inception and it’s execution, utilizing audio and graphics programming, web technologies, and physical fabrication to build systems that explore my relationship with tedium, improvisation, and the world around me.
