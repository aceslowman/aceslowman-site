Future / In Progress

One difficulty I've had with VR is having clear solution for how to share the art. The most defining characteristics of the medium are flattened when the art created is eventually rendered into an image. On top of that, the technology itself is still incredibly inaccessible, although some progress has been made recently as hardware has become cheaper.

What I look forward to in future experiments is to utilize VR in more live environments, either as an expressive controller for another performer, or as some aspect of an installation.
