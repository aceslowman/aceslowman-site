Θ (2018)

A simple monophonic synthesizer built for Ableton, using Max for Live.

I wanted to put together a synthesizer with which harmony could be explored through geometry, allowing for complex sounds to come from the arrangement of points around a circle.

The synthesis is implements multiple carrier fm synthesis. It began as an experiment that I built for the browser, which can be found here
